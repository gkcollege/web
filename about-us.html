About Us

Why choose us?

We 'Gurukul Keshari College' proud of our teaching faculty, who, on the one hand nurture the thirst for higher knowledge among students and on the other hand become their mentors for life. Students reciprocate this affection and this can be seen in their interaction and performance in curricular as well as extra- curricular activities. Our college can also boast of strong outreach programs that enable youngsters to reach out to the disadvantaged sections of society and take meaningful steps towards bringing about positive changes. The backbone of the college, namely, our non-teaching staff, relentlessly strive to ensure the smooth running of the institution.

With classrooms equipped with LCD projectors and whiteboards, a fully loaded gymnasium, an auditorium, an open air theatre, a library, an audio visual room and computer labs, we offer higher education to our wards in an atmosphere of ambience and technology. The programs conducted by us include B.A./M.A Not just what the University prescribes, we also cater to the all-round development of learners by offering nine value-added and skill-based programs. None of the above would have been possible without the support and guidance of the Management whose silent and strong backing ensure that our achievements move constantly vertically and speedily upwards. Taking the liberty to borrow from Rabindranath Tagore, I wish to ascertain that Gurukul aims to create young citizens whose minds are without fear and whose heads are held high.

Who we are?

Greeting's from team Gurukul. Gurukul Keshari Shikshan Sansthan Samiti Bandanwada was started on 15th June 2007 under the full guidance of Marwar Group of Education Director Shri Jagdish Prasad Chhangani and Co-Director Shri Babulalji Bishnoi, Makrana District. To provide best quality education and hostel facility to the students of rural areas around Bandanwara and primary level to the students .With the aim of providing education from primary level to postgraduate level, and starting primary school in 2007, the educational institution obtained secondary level recognition in 2009. In 2011, the higher secondary level was recognized by the Education Department, Government of Rajasthan in all three faculties. And since 2011, the all-round educational and intellectual development of the students of rural areas by Gurukul Kesari Public Senior Secondary School, Bandanwada.The institute is committed to give good examination results in future itself. In the session 2018-19, under the guidance of Mr. Director Mr. Jagdish Prasad ji Chhangani and co-director Mr. Babulal ji Vishnoi, the institute has requested the College Commissionerate, Government of Rajasthan for conducting college level classes. Received TNOC and currently BA & B.Sc. 1st year 2nd year classes are being conducted by the institute.Paramedical courses by the institute in future Entire team is striving to get accreditation of D Pharma B Pharma and Nursing Paramedical courses.With the blessings of Shri Balaji Maharaj and the guidance of Director and Co-Director, success will come and the institute will play its important role in providing employment education to the students in rural areas.
